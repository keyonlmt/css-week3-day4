Here are some facts about birds:

Birds Can Fly High

Some birds, like the Bar-Headed Goose, can fly at altitudes of over 20,000 feet.

They Have Incredible Vision

Many birds have vision that is 3-4 times sharper than humans, allowing them to spot prey from afar.

Some birds can mimic human speech

Parrots, especially African Greys, are known for their ability to imitate human voices and even understand simple commands.
